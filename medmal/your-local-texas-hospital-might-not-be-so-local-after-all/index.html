Our alphabetical listing shows who owns every hospital in Texas, and where the owners are located

Robert Painter
April 5, 2017

Although we are based in Houston, the medical malpractice lawyers at Painter Law Firm represent patients and their families in cases against hospitals all over Texas. We handle anesthesia error, surgical error, birth injury, brain injury, sepsis, bariatric, lap band, gastric bypass, infection, and many other types of medical negligence cases.

Clients from many cities and towns across Texas have shared with us their belief that local attorneys are scared to file a lawsuit against local hospitals. Before reaching Painter Law Firm, some of our clients were turned down by multiple local lawyers who said they did not want to tackle a hometown hospital at the courthouse.

When we start representing a new client, one of the things we do is research the hospital involved and who actually owns it. Over our many years of handling medical malpractice cases in Texas, we have found that many hospitals are owned and operated by far-away, out-of-state big corporations.
