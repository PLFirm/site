Robert Painter and Taunya Painter recognized among &quot;Houston's Top Lawyers&quot;

H Texas magazine's prestigious July 2012 recognizes the firm's attorneys for their medical malpractice work

Robert Painter
July 27, 2012

In July 2012, H Texas magazine recognized Painter Law Firm attorneys Robert Painter and Taunya Painter as being among “Houston’s Top Lawyers” for their significant work in plaintiff’s medical malpractice cases.

H Texas magazine selects its picks for “Top Lawyers” from peer votes and reader nominations. The votes are tabulated, backgrounds are checked, and the list is narrowed down to the top 2% of lawyers voted as the best in the Houston area.

Attorney Robert Painter said, “Unfortunately, the Texas tort reform law threw out the baby with the bath water. We at Painter Law Firm are proud to still represent clients in serious medical malpractice cases, and are grateful for the vote of confidence in the recent H Texas magazine recognition.”
