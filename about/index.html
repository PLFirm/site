Hard Work

Every day we help real people who have suffered catastrophic injuries or death because of medical negligence. We have significant litigation experience in medical malpractice and brain injury lawsuits.

Solid Resources

We are not intimidated or afraid to take on any hospital, doctor or company that has harmed our clients.

We put our legal know-how, financial resources, extensive background, and big law firm experience to fight for great results for our clients.

Areas We Serve

We serve every city in the state of Texas for patients who have suffered from medical malpractice, inlcuding the metro areas of Austin, San Antonio, Dallas, Fort Worth, and Houston.

Respect

Every year since 2008, the Better Business Bureau of Greater Houston and South Texas (BBB) has honored Painter Law Firm with its Award of Distinction. Most recently, the BBB recognized the Firm’s commitment to top notch customer service, ethics, and professionalism.

Attorney Robert Painter has been named a Super Lawyer by Thomson Reuters from 2019. 2023, and a member of Houston Bar Association board of directors. He has served in leadership positions in multiple bar organizations, including most notably as editor-in-chief ofThe Houston Lawyer magazine and on the editorial board of the Texas Bar Journal. Since 2011, he has held the prestigious AV preeminent rating from Martindale Hubbell, the highest peer rating standard for legal expertise, communication skills, and ethical standards. He has a 10/10 rating by Avvo.

Attorney Taunya Painter is the past president of the Houston Northwest Bar Association, and a previous editor-in-chief of The Houston Lawyer magazine. She has a 10/10 rating by Avvo.
